Frequently Asked

Questions & Answers

The minimum investment depends on your chosen plan. The Starter plan accepts deposits from as little as $500 USD. The Growth plan starts at $5,000, Premium at $25,000, and our Institutional tier is available from $100,000. You can always upgrade to a higher plan by topping up your investment.
The lock-in period is the minimum duration your capital must remain invested to earn the stated rate. The Starter plan has no lock-in. Growth requires 90 days, and Premium requires 6 months. Early withdrawal during a lock-in period is permitted but incurs a 2% early exit fee and forfeiture of any unpaid interest for that period. After the lock-in expires, funds can be withdrawn freely with 3–5 business days processing.
Returns are credited to your AUREX wallet on the 1st of each calendar month for monthly payouts. Quarterly bonuses for the Growth plan are credited at the end of each quarter (March 31, June 30, September 30, December 31). You can withdraw credited returns to your linked bank account at any time, or enable auto-reinvestment for compounding growth.
Tax obligations vary by your country of residence. AUREX provides detailed annual tax statements for all investors summarizing total interest earned during the year. You are responsible for declaring investment income per your local tax laws. Our Premium and Institutional clients have access to tax advisory sessions with our partner tax professionals to structure investments efficiently.
Client capital is held in segregated custodian accounts with licensed banking partners — entirely separate from AUREX operational funds. All client deposits carry institutional insurance coverage. Our platform undergoes annual third-party security and compliance audits. However, all investments carry inherent risk. We encourage all investors to read our full Risk Disclosure document before committing capital.
