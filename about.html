Our Company

We believe everyone
deserves dollar wealth

AUREX was founded on a single conviction: access to stable, high-yield dollar investments shouldn't be reserved for the ultra-wealthy.

Democratizing
Dollar
Investments
for the world.

AUREX was born from a simple observation: millions of people living in emerging economies watch their savings erode to inflation and currency devaluation — while institutional investors quietly compound wealth in dollar-denominated assets on the other side of the world.

We built AUREX to close that gap. By combining institutional investment infrastructure with a digital-first platform, we've made it possible for anyone with $500 and an internet connection to access the same dollar yields that family offices and hedge funds have enjoyed for decades.

Today, AUREX manages over $2.4 billion in assets for more than 18,000 investors across 47 countries. We remain committed to our founding principle: financial growth should not be a privilege of the few.

Our Journey

Five Years of Quiet Excellence

From a founding team of four in a Lagos co-working space to one of the most trusted alternative investment platforms in Africa and the Middle East.

2019 — Founded

The Beginning

AUREX is founded by Emeka Osei and three co-founders in Lagos, Nigeria. The founding vision: build a dollar investment platform accessible to everyday Africans. First $5M in assets raised from angel investors and early adopters within three months of soft launch.

2020 — First Major Milestone

$100M Under Management

Despite global economic uncertainty, AUREX demonstrates its resilience by crossing $100 million in managed assets — ahead of projections. Expands operations to Kenya, Ghana, and the UAE. Regulatory approvals obtained in three jurisdictions.

2022 — Community Scale

10,000 Investors Milestone

AUREX reaches 10,000 active investors, spanning 28 countries. Launches the Growth and Premium plan tiers. Introduces the investor dashboard and mobile-optimized interface. First institutional partner — a Dubai-based family office — commits $50M to the platform.

2024 — The $2B Era

$2B Assets Under Management

AUREX crosses the $2 billion AUM mark — a landmark that places the platform among the most significant independent dollar investment platforms globally. 18,000+ investors across 47 countries. Launches Institutional tier and dedicated advisory services. Prepares for Series B funding.
